Hugo Daniel

I am a software developer based in Amadora, Portugal.

Working in multidisciplinary teams where all ideas are valued is what I look for in my day-to-day projects. With an open mind I seek to find solutions where every voice is heard in an attempt to make whole and balanced products.

At the moment I am working with the amazing team of journalists on Wilder , a news website dedicated to nature and biodiversity related themes and stories.

On my spare time I am remaking an old personal project launched in 2017 - a design tool where you can make pixel art and patterns inspired on Portuguese tiles.

Previously I worked at Sketch , Farfetch, a bunch of startups and in several projects for the Portuguese government.
